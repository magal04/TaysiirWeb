Book Review: BOOK Of DOOR

Posted on April 20, 2023 by book Plaza

Novel passes the time if you've got nothing else to read. The premise is decent in a silly way...; obviously a story based on magic books is going to have to work pretty hard to suspend disbelief effectively enough to be engaging. This novel doesn't ever succeed in that. The bad guys are basically characterized by using curse words and only the bad guys use this language. Indeed, shallow character development is the main reason the novel is unmemorable. It is not very well written in general. The writer wants to be C.S. Lewis, but doesn't even make it to J. K. Rowling's skill level.

Book Review: NOT A RIVER

Posted on May 14, 2023 by book Plaza

Not A River, translated from Spanish by McDermott, tells the story of two men...,Enero and El Negro, who embark on a fishing trip on a nameless South American river with a teenager named Tilo, the son of their late friend Eusebio. After spending hours struggling to reel in a stingray, they end up shooting it before hanging it from a tree and letting it rot.
